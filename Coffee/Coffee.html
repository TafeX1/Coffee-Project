Caramel Macchiato

A popular coffee beverage characterized by its distinct layers and sweet finish. The Caramel Macchiato is prepared by combining steamed milk and vanilla-flavored syrup, which is then "marked" by pouring shots of rich espresso through the foam. It is traditionally finished with a signature crosshatch drizzle of buttery caramel sauce. Unlike a standard latte where the espresso is added first, the macchiato method ensures that the consumer sips the bold coffee through the creamy, sweet foam, creating a flavor profile that evolves with every sip.

Vanilla cappuccino

A smooth and aromatic variation of the classic Italian coffee, the Vanilla Cappuccino is crafted by layering rich espresso with steamed milk and a generous topping of airy milk foam. It is distinguished by the infusion of sweet vanilla syrup, which mellows the intensity of the espresso while enhancing its natural roasted notes. Traditionally, this beverage maintains an equal balance of foam, milk, and coffee, often finished with a light dusting of cinnamon or cocoa powder to complement the fragrant vanilla aroma.

Mocha

A decadent fusion of rich espresso and chocolate, It is prepared by mixing a shot of espresso with chocolate, followed by smooth steamed milk, creating a balanced blend of bitterness and sweetness. Often considered a bridge between hot chocolate and coffee, it is traditionally served topped with a thick layer of whipped cream or milk foam and a garnish of chocolate shavings or a drizzle of cocoa syrup.

cortado

A Spanish-inspired coffee classic consisting of equal parts espresso and warm, silky steamed milk. The Cortado is designed to strike a perfect balance, where the milk reduces the acidity of the espresso without masking its robust flavor or body. Unlike a cappuccino, it contains very little to no foam, resulting in a smooth, dense texture. It is traditionally served in a small glass, highlighting the harmonious 1:1 ratio that makes it a favorite for those who appreciate the strength of coffee with a mellow, creamy finish.
